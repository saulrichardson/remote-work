\section*{Overview}
We estimate impulse response functions (IRFs) for individual productivity (percentile) following firm-level hiring growth by role. We split results across four groups: Non-Remote/Non-Startup, Remote/Non-Startup, Non-Remote/Startup, and Remote/Startup. Estimates come from local projections with user\texttimes{}firm and time fixed effects and firm-clustered standard errors.

\vspace{0.5em}
This document assembles the per-role IRF plots output to the results folders. Generate plots with:\\
\texttt{do spec/composition\\_irfs\\_by	extrm{\_}remote	extrm{\_}startup.do} then\\
\texttt{do spec/plot	extrm{\_}irf	extrm{\_}graphs	extrm{\_}by	extrm{\_}remote	extrm{\_}startup.do}
