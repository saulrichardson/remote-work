\section*{Crunchbase Fundraising: Robustness Checks}

\paragraph{Purpose.}
This note documents robustness checks for the canonical Crunchbase fundraising specification under firm fixed effects and half-year fixed effects.
We report both OLS and IV estimates of the Remote$\times$Post coefficient.

\paragraph{Robustness dimensions.}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Top-tail sensitivity}: winsorization / trimming of the USD-raised distribution within half-year (conditional on positive USD).
  \item \textbf{Placebo}: re-define ``Post'' in the pre-Covid period (fake cutoffs) and re-run the same IV design.
  \item \textbf{Extensive margin dollars}: use $1\{\text{USD raised} > 0\}$ as the outcome (amounts are often undisclosed).
  \item \textbf{Controls}: cohort-style controls and a firm-performance ``horse race'' using employment growth.
\end{itemize}
