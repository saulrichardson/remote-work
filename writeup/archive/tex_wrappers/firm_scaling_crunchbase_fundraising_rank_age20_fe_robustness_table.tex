\section*{Crunchbase Fundraising: USD Raised Rank (Age $<20$ Robustness)}

\paragraph{Outcome.}
\textbf{USD raised rank} is the within-half-year rank (1--100) of USD raised.

\paragraph{Sample.}
Crunchbase-matched private firms (drop public firms). Column~(4) restricts to firm-half-year observations with age $<20$; columns~(1)--(3) use the full matched-private sample.

\paragraph{Columns.}
Columns~(1)--(3) absorb firm fixed effects and replace half-year fixed effects with industry$\times$half-year and/or HQ-state$\times$half-year shocks:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Column~(1): industry$\times$half-year shocks
  \item Column~(2): HQ-state$\times$half-year shocks
  \item Column~(3): both sets of shocks
\end{itemize}
Column~(4) returns to firm and half-year fixed effects and restricts the sample to age $<20$.
