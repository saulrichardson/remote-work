\section*{Crunchbase Fundraising: USD Raised (FE Robustness)}

\paragraph{Sample.}
The unit of observation is firm$\times$half-year. We restrict to Crunchbase-matched private firms (drop public firms).
Column~(4) further restricts to firm-half-year observations with age $<20$.

\paragraph{Columns.}
Columns~(1)--(3) absorb firm fixed effects and replace half-year fixed effects with time-varying shocks:
\begin{itemize}
  \item Column~(1): industry$\times$half-year shocks
  \item Column~(2): HQ-state$\times$half-year shocks
  \item Column~(3): both sets of shocks
\end{itemize}
Column~(4) returns to firm and half-year fixed effects and restricts to age $<20$.

\paragraph{Notes.}
Panels~A and B report OLS and IV estimates of $\text{Remote}\times \mathds{1}(\text{Post})$.
The IV instruments with $\text{Teleworkable}\times \mathds{1}(\text{Post})$ and standard errors are clustered by firm.
