\section*{Crunchbase: Cohort Fixed Effects Robustness}

\paragraph{Sample.}
The unit of observation is firm$\times$half-year. We restrict to firms matched to Crunchbase and drop public firms
(fundraising outcomes are not comparable for public companies).

\paragraph{Specification.}
We estimate the effect of $ \text{Remote} \times \mathds{1}(\text{Post}) $ (instrumented by $ \text{Teleworkable} \times \mathds{1}(\text{Post}) $)
under two alternative fixed-effect structures, both \emph{excluding} startup terms:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Baseline}: firm FE $\times$ half-year FE.
  \item \textbf{Cohort}: firm FE $\times$ (founding cohort in 5-year bins)$\times$(calendar year) FE.
\end{itemize}
