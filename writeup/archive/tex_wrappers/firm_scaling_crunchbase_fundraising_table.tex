\section*{Crunchbase}

\paragraph{Sample.}
The unit of observation is firm$\times$half-year. We restrict to firms matched to Crunchbase and drop public firms
(fundraising outcomes are not comparable for public companies). We do not restrict to startup-only firms in the baseline sample.

\paragraph{Outcomes.}
We construct half-year outcomes from Crunchbase funding rounds:
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Any round}: indicator for at least one round in the half-year.
  \item \textbf{Repeat fundraising}: indicators for more than one and more than two rounds in the half-year.
  \item \textbf{Dollars}: $\log(1+\text{USD raised})$ in the half-year.
  \item \textbf{Rank dollars}: within-half-year percentile bin (q100) of USD raised (skew-robust).
\end{itemize}
