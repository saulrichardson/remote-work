\section*{Crunchbase Fundraising: Core Outcomes}

\paragraph{Sample.}
The unit of observation is firm$\times$half-year. We restrict to firms matched to Crunchbase and drop public firms
(fundraising outcomes are not comparable for public companies).

\paragraph{Outcomes.}
\begin{itemize}
  \item \textbf{Any USD raised}: indicator for positive USD raised in the half-year (includes seed).
  \item \textbf{Series A+ round}: indicator for any Series A-or-higher round in the half-year.
  \item \textbf{USD raised rank}: within-half-year percentile bin (1--100) of USD raised (skew-robust).
  \item \textbf{USD raised}: USD raised in levels in the half-year (millions; includes zeros).
\end{itemize}
