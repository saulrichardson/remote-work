\section*{Crunchbase Fundraising: Does Including Startup$\times$Post (var4) Matter?}

\paragraph{Context.}
Under firm fixed effects and half-year fixed effects, Remote and Post are absorbed, so the identifying term
is Remote$\times$Post. A question is whether we should also control for Startup$\times$Post (var4), allowing
startups to have a different post-period shift in fundraising outcomes.
