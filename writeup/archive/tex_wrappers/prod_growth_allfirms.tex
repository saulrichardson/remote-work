\section*{Productivity vs Growth (Composition)}

\begin{itemize}
  \item Sample: all firms with pre/post data; startups = age \(\le 10\); \(N=1{,}774\).
  \item Outcome: change in average worker rank (composition-inclusive); captures incumbent shifts plus selection/upgrading.
  \item Spec: \(\Delta\text{Prod}^{\text{level}} = \alpha + \beta\,\Delta\text{Growth} + \gamma\,\text{Startup}\).
  \item Coefficient: \(\beta=5.44\), p = 0.019.
\end{itemize}
